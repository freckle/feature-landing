Front Row Science

Evolution

Grades 6-8

Unit Overview

Throughout this hands-on unit, students will explore genetic variation and biological evolution as they develop scientific literacy, inquiry, communication, and collaboration. By creating their own species to track throughout the unit, students will understand the process of natural selection.

LESSON 4: Evidence of Evolution

100 mins

Evidence of Evolution

Students will analyze evidence for evolution, focusing on fossils and other anatomical evidence. Using this evidence, students will create a hypothesis for the order in which adaptations took place in different organisms. They will then learn to represent these evolutionary relationships in trees called cladograms.
