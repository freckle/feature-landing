The Revolutionary War

Lesson Overview

Through this 10-week unit, students will interact with the events surrounding the Revolutionary War by engaging in classroom discussions and debates, analyzing primary sources, and evaluating different perspectives. As students deepen their understanding of this pivotal time in American history, they will also build standards-aligned ELA skills through leveled texts that allow all readers to engage with the material. The unit will culminate in a test that will test for understanding about the American Revolution.
