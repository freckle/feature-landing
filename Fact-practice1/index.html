What Front Row Fact Practice looks like...

Front Row's Fact Practice was created to improve student's math foundation through basic math drills. This foundation is crucial for allowing them to later solve more complex problems.

Students choose Fact Practice

Students have the option to do Fact Practice up to two times a day. This is to ensure that the focus stays on conceptual learning with Front Row's adaptive and assigned math practice.

Students practice fact fluency

Front Row Fact Practice is NOT timed—research shows that timing students while they practice math problems can create anxiety and an aversion to math.

Students earn coins on their way to mastery

They can use their coins to purchase clothing and accessories for their avatar. They only advance to the next level when they demonstrate mastery, or receive 3 perfect scores on their current level.
