How Front Row is standards-aligned...

We know that for teachers, it's important to have effective tools that allow students to be comfortable when taking standardized tests. To this end, Front Row includes question formats seen in many standardized tests and aligned to common standards so you can be sure students are set up for success.

Standards-aligned ELA

Front Row's ELA articles are created to address specific anchor skills for students to practice. Articles are also created for different genres, with many aligning to Science and Social Studies standards as well.

Standards-aligned math

Students practice questions divided into standards-aligned domains, and teachers receive detailed data reports that make it easy to quickly understand student performance by each domain. Students can practice at their own level through Adaptive practice or on a specific domain with Assigned practice.

Inquiry based lessons

In Front Row's innovative real-world lessons, students take a deep dive into math concepts. The objective of each lesson aligns with specific standards and objectives, and many lessons also align with various Science and Social Studies standards.
