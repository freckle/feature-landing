What using Assigned Practice looks like...

Front Row's Assigned Practice for math makes it easy for teachers to create assignments for students. Teachers can pick the domain and standard they would like students to practice, and and specify which students should receive the assignment.

Teachers assign a standard for students

Standards are available for grades K - 8. Front Row will then pull questions from that specific domain and standard and create assignments for all of the students specified.

Students answer questions on the specified topic

Teachers can confidentally create assignments for students, knowing that Front Row's questions are standards-aligned and come in formats seen in the major standardized tests.

Teachers get detailed data reports

As students practice, teachers can access actionable data views that show exactly how each student is doing. This real-time feedback allows teachers to address student pain points as they happen, rather than having to wait until after tests are graded to identify where students are struggling.
