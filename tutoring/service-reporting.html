Session reporting

Following every tutoring session, tutors send the student and parents a detailed report. This report includes what articles were read and how the student did in specific skills in Speaking, Reading, and Writing. It shows areas the student excels in as well as the areas that they can improve in. Then, it explains what the next few tutoring sessions will look like, including what articles will be read and strategies for building strengths and addressing the areas of improvement.
